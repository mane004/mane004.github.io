Our Story

____

The idea for WardrobeGenius was born out of a personal challenge: the everyday dilemma of not knowing what to wear from one's own closet. We wanted to create a simple, intelligent solution that helps people better understand their style and make efficient use of their wardrobe. WardrobeGenius was founded with the mission of merging technology and fashion into a user-friendly app that makes getting dressed a little easier every day.
